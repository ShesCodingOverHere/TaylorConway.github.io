## P1–P2 Sperm Competition Assay

- Sequential mating design to quantify relative paternity success (P1 vs P2)  
- Controlled mating timeline with standardized fly age and conditions  
- Offspring scored by phenotype to assign paternity and estimate competitive fertilization success  

[Sperm Competition Protocol]({{ site.baseurl }}/files/P1-P2spermcompetition.pdf)

## XO–XY Competition Cages

- Replicated population cages tracking genotype frequency dynamics over time  
- Controlled starting composition with equal representation of XO and XY individuals  
- Periodic sampling and PCR-based genotyping to quantify changes in genotype frequency

[XO-XY Competition Cages Protocol]({{ site.baseurl }}/files/XOvXYcompetitionCages.pdf)

## Experimental Evolution Cages

- Long-term population cages used to track evolutionary dynamics under controlled conditions  
- Structured genetic backgrounds with defined male genotypes and diverse female lines  
- Regular sampling and integration with downstream assays (e.g., sperm competition) to monitor evolutionary change

[Experimental Evolution Cages Protocol]({{ site.baseurl }}/files/Experimentalevolutionprotocol.pdf)
